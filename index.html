Kedarkantha

stands out as a highly sought-after winter trek, earning it the title of "Winter Trek Queen." Its snowy landscapes, breathtaking vistas from the summit, and the simplicity of its trails have made it a beloved choice among trekkers. This relatively gentle route winds through the dense pine forests of Govind National Park, sparing trekkers from excessive physical strain. Reaching an altitude of 12,500 ft, the views from the top are truly worth the ascent. Embarking on the Kedarkantha trek promises a one-of-a-kind experience, with sights that include the expansive night sky, lush meadows, snow-covered pathways, charming villages, fragrant pine forests, towering peaks that seem to touch the heavens, serene rivers, and echoes of ancient mythological tales.

Kedarkantha Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Dehradun to Sankri | Route to The Paradise of Himalayas

On the first day of your Kedarkantha trek, you'll start your journey from Dehradun and head towards the beautiful village of Sankri, often referred to as the "Paradise of Himalayas."

Day 2: Sankri to Juda Ka Talab | Where Lord Shiva once came to meditate

Today, you'll trek from Sankri to Juda Ka Talab, a serene lake where it is believed that Lord Shiva once came to meditate.

Day 3: Juda Ka Talab to Base Camp | Get your Cameras ready

Prepare to capture the breathtaking scenery as you hike from Juda Ka Talab to the Base Camp of Kedarkantha.

Day 4: Base Camp to Kedar Kantha Summit | Descend Back to Juda Ka Talab

The highlight of your trek, as you ascend to the Kedar Kantha Summit. Later, descend back to Juda Ka Talab for the night.

Day 5: Descend From Juda Ka Talab to Sankri | Drive Back to Dehradun | Departure

On the final day, you'll descend from Juda Ka Talab to Sankri and then drive back to Dehradun. It's time to bid farewell to this incredible trekking experience.
